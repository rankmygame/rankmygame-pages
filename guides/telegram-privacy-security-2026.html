Telegram Privacy & Security Guide 2026 — Protect Your Account and Data

Essential privacy and security settings for Telegram. Account protection, privacy controls, two-step verification, and best practices for safe communication.

Why Telegram Privacy Matters

Telegram's default settings allow significant information sharing. By default, your phone number is visible to contacts, your "last seen" timestamp is accessible, and your profile photo can be viewed by anyone. Understanding and adjusting these settings is essential for maintaining privacy.

Essential Security Settings

Enable Two-Step Verification

Two-step verification adds a password to your account in addition to your phone number. This prevents unauthorized access even if someone obtains your SMS code.

How to enable: Settings → Privacy and Security → Two-Step Verification → Set Additional Password

Set Up a Recovery Email

Linking a recovery email allows you to reset your password if you forget it and provides a backup access method.

How to set up: Settings → Privacy and Security → Two-Step Verification → Set Recovery Email

Active Sessions Management

Regularly review and terminate sessions on devices you don't recognize or no longer use.

How to manage: Settings → Privacy and Security → Active Sessions → Terminate individual sessions or "Terminate All Other Sessions"

Privacy Controls

Phone Number Privacy

Control who can see your phone number. Options include: Everyone, My Contacts, or Nobody. For maximum privacy, set to "My Contacts" or "Nobody" and add exceptions as needed.

Last Seen & Online Status

Choose who can see when you were last active. Options include: Everyone, My Contacts, or Nobody. Using "Nobody" gives you complete invisibility about your activity status.

Profile Photo & Bio

Control visibility of your profile photo and bio. Set to "My Contacts" to limit visibility to people you know.

Forwarded Messages

When you forward a message, recipients can tap through to your profile. Disable this if you want to prevent people from finding your profile through forwarded content.

Group & Channel Privacy

Control who can add you to groups and channels. Setting to "My Contacts" prevents strangers from adding you to unwanted groups.

Account Security Best Practices

  • Use a strong, unique password — Different from any other account
  • Never share your verification code — Telegram staff will never ask for it
  • Be cautious of clone apps — Only download Telegram from official app stores
  • Enable auto-delete — Set messages to automatically delete after a period
  • Use Secret Chats for sensitive content — End-to-end encrypted and device-specific
  • Regularly review linked devices — Remove devices you don't recognize

What to Do If Your Account Is Compromised

  1. Terminate all active sessions immediately
  2. Enable two-step verification if not already active
  3. Change your recovery email if it was modified
  4. Contact Telegram support if you can't regain access
  5. Inform your contacts that your account was compromised
